On June 1, 2026, Yann LeCun published a new research paper titled 'When Does LeJEPA Learn a World Model?', proving that under Gaussian latent dynamics, LeJEPA can recover the hidden state behind nonlinear observations up to rotation. The key insight is that linear latent features are the most stable across nearby views while nonlinear features decay faster, causing the objective to naturally select the real-world variables. The guarantee holds under specific Gaussian latent assumptions and represents a formal theoretical foundation for the Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture family of models.
